Visual Query System Of Massive Taxi Trajectory Data

A novel visual query system of massive taxi trajectory data by utilizing the power of text search engine. It directly supports various queries by text based conditions and no special spatial data structure is needed to manage trajectories.

Efficient and user-friendly visualizations are designed to display taxi trips information, discover illegal taxi behaviors and extract popular pick-up, drop-off locations with D3.js

Visualizing Hidden Themes of Taxi Movement with Semantic Transformation

A new methodology is developed to discover and analyze the hidden knowledge of massive taxi trajectory data within a city. This approach creatively transforms the geographic coordinates (i.e. latitude and longitude) to street names reflecting contextual semantic information.

The system integrates interactive visualization tools, including taxi topic maps, topic routes, street clouds and parallel coordinates with D3.js, to visualize the probability-based topical information.
